Fuzzy Peaches

About Fuzzy Peaches

Fuzzy Peaches are peach-flavored gummy candies that are coated with a layer of sugar to give them a slightly fuzzy texture, hence the name. They typically have a soft, chewy consistency and are often brightly colored to resemble real peaches.

Interesting Facts

  • Although primarily produced by Maynards, they are also produced by other comapnies.
  • The candy is designed to fade from yellow to red, just like peaches.
  • Fuzzy Peaches are a popular candy in North America, particularly in the Canada.
  • The candy has a sweet flavor that is loved by adults and kids alike.
